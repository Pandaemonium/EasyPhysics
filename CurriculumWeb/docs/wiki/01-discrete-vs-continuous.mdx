# Discrete vs. Continuous Symmetries

To understand the universe, we must start with the most fundamental concept: **symmetry**. 

## Everyday Symmetries
Think of a square. It possesses **discrete rotational symmetry**. If you close your eyes and someone rotates it by exactly 90 degrees, it looks identical when you open your eyes. But if they rotate it by 45 degrees, you can tell it changed.

Now think of a perfect circle. It possesses **continuous rotational symmetry**. No matter how tiny the angle of rotation—even a billionth of a degree—it remains visually unchanged. 

In physics, continuous symmetries are the most profound because they are inextricably linked to motion and the conservation of physical properties.
